Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

What file formats are included?

Each product comes with a variety of file formats to ensure compatibility with most design software. Typically, you'll find PNG, JPG, PSD, and AI files. Please check the individual product description for specific details.

How do I receive my files after purchase?

Once your purchase is complete, you will receive an email with a secure download link. You can download your files instantly. The link will be active for 30 days.

What is your refund policy?

Due to the digital nature of our products, all sales are final and we do not offer refunds. However, if you encounter any issues with your files, please contact our support team, and we'll be happy to assist you.

Can I use these assets for commercial projects?

Yes! All our products come with a standard commercial license, which allows you to use them in an unlimited number of personal and commercial projects. However, you are not allowed to resell or redistribute the original files.
